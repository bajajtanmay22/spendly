Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic logs such as login timestamps and page visits.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Service:

  • To display your expense history, summaries, and reports.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality obligations. These providers are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us via the information on our website. We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries within a reasonable time.
